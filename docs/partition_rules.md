\# Haryana Partition Manager (HPM)



\# Business Rules Specification



Version 1.0



\---



\# Purpose



This document defines the business rules that govern partition proceedings.



Unlike the workflow specification, these rules are invariant. Every algorithm, validation routine and report generated by HPM must comply with them.



\---



\# General Rules



\## BR-001



Every partition belongs to exactly one village.



\---



\## BR-002



Every partition is based upon exactly one Jamabandi.



\---



\## BR-003



The selected Jamabandi becomes read-only.



It must never be modified.



\---



\## BR-004



Generated records never overwrite source records.



\---



\# Ownership Rules



\## BR-101



Every generated Khewat must have at least one owner.



\---



\## BR-102



Owner shares must total 100%.



\---



\## BR-103



No owner may receive a negative share.



\---



\## BR-104



Ownership percentages shall be calculated using configurable precision.



\---



\# Parcel Rules



\## BR-201



Every parcel must belong to one generated Khewat.



\---



\## BR-202



No parcel may exist twice.



\---



\## BR-203



Every original parcel must either



• remain unchanged



or



• be completely partitioned.



\---



\## BR-204



Split parcels must preserve total area.



\---



\# Area Rules



\## BR-301



Total generated area must equal total original area.



\---



\## BR-302



Village area must remain constant.



\---



\## BR-303



Every generated Khewat area equals the sum of its parcels.



\---



\# Validation Rules



\## BR-401



No validation error may be ignored.



\---



\## BR-402



Generation cannot begin until validation succeeds.



\---



\## BR-403



Validation must be repeatable.



\---



\# Generation Rules



\## BR-501



Generation shall be deterministic.



Running generation twice with identical input shall produce identical output.



\---



\## BR-502



Generated Khewat numbers must follow configurable numbering rules.



\---



\## BR-503



Generation must never modify source Jamabandi records.



\---



\# Audit Rules



\## BR-601



Every significant operation shall be recorded.



\---



\## BR-602



Generation shall be reproducible.



\---



\## BR-603



Reports must be reproducible from saved data.
